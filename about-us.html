About Us

At Mac & Me, we offer gourmet Macaroni and Cheese delivered straight to your door. As students ourselves, we know that late-night eating is common and dare we say it, inevitable. Some days, it’s hard to find food that’s filling but that won’t make you fall into a “food coma.” That’s where we come in.

Our Mac n Cheese comes in a variety of flavors because we want to appeal to everyone’s tastes. We have everything from lighter, vegan options to heartier, flavor-bursting dishes. All of our meals are made with locally-sourced dairy and Mac & Me’s specialty cheese blends.

Our Mac n Cheese is perfect to eat individually or with friends and is especially great when studying or hanging out. Our dishes can work as a quick and easy meal or a filling late-night snack.
Delivery food can get expensive. That’s why we offer cost-friendly prices with students and the local community in mind. Satisfy your cheese cravings today without breaking the bank.
